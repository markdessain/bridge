Walmer Castle Bridge Group

A friendly bridge grouped based in the Notting Hill area. We provide weekly Wednesday evening supervised duplicate sessions.

It is a perfect evening for players who know the rules of the game and want to play in a social but competitive environment. You do not need to come with a partner.

The Calander and Results can be found on the Ruff Club website.
